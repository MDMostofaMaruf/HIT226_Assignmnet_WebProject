About CDU CODERS CLUB


The CDU coders club started its journey in 2012 with an initiative to bring the CDU’s IT professionals closer . We had an aim to provide support and share the best of skills amongst the community. Over the years the club had heavily diversified into activities of various sort including providing coaching by the industry leaders and arranging projects with the prime IT companies to brighten future career opportunities for our valued members. We also act as an Information Technonogy company and an industry leader in the Northern Territory in providing excellent softwares and applications for our clienteles.

A B M Zafrul Huda, Co-Founder, Joint Chair and Head of Public Relations

Zafrul has an amazing personality and a style to match it. He is a CDU graduate with exceptionally well proven track record within the media and public relations arena. He has had worked with various high profile Information technology projects with Big4 companies such as, Ernest Young and Delloite. Zafrul always aims to provide his fullest support in proving a positive image of the club to the greater audience.

MD Mostofa Maruf, Co-Founder and Vice Chair and Head of Technology

Maruf is an exceptionally talented individual who completed his bachelor of IT from CDU. He has had an amazing portfolio working with the top level IT companies including Microsoft and Google. Maruf’s prime objective in our club is to train our associates to the fullest and help blossom the young individuals.

Fahim Kawsar, Co-Founder, Joint Chair and Head of Operations

Fahim is an enthusiastic individual with extensive operation, project management and implementation experience within the best software companies around the world such as SAP and Oracle. Fahim’s prime objective of the company is to sake sure of the swift operations and management of the club. Fahim hopes to collaborate even further with the government agencies worldwide to shorten the skill gap and enhance the coding quality to the furthest.
